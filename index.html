Welcome to TEDxCornell! We are a vibrant community of innovators, creators, and dreamers from Cornell University and the surrounding region. We come together to share ideas, inspire each other, and create a better future. We are passionate about exploring new ways of thinking, pushing boundaries, and making a real difference. From talks to workshops, events to collaborations, TEDxCornell is a platform for learning, connecting, and growing. Join us and discover the power of ideas!

Apply as a Speaker

Do you have an idea worth spreading? The TEDxCornell stage is the perfect place to share it! Apply today to potentially get selected as a speaker for our next event!

Apply as a Member

Our team application process is currently done for the semester. Check back in the Spring for our next recruiting cycle!

Contact us by Email

If you are interested in partnering with TEDxCornell, or if you have additional questions about the club, please email us at tedx@cornell.edu. We will respond to you as soon as possible.

Follow us on Social Media

For updates on our events and the application process, follow us on Instagram or Facebook. For more information about our family, connect with us on LinkedIn.
