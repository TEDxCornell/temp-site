Welcome to TEDxCornell! We are a vibrant community of innovators, creators, and dreamers from Cornell University and the surrounding region. We come together to share ideas, inspire each other, and create a better future. We are passionate about exploring new ways of thinking, pushing boundaries, and making a real difference. From talks to workshops, events to collaborations, TEDxCornell is a platform for learning, connecting, and growing. Join us and discover the power of ideas!
